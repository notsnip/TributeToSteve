Steven Paul Jobs

1955-2011

Steven Paul Jobs, also known as "Steve Jobs" was a charismatic pioneer of the personal computer era. With Steve Wozniak, Jobs founded Apple Inc. in 1976 and transformed the company into a world leader in telecommunications. Widely considered a visionary and a genius, he oversaw the launch of such revolutionary products as the iPod and the iPhone.

Contributions

  • Pioneered the personal computer revolution with Apple Computer.
  • Made computers easier to use with the graphical user interface (GUI).
  • Popularized digital music with the iPod.
  • Revolutionized the smartphone with the iPhone.
  • Made animation more accessible with Pixar Animation Studios.
